Blueberry Pie

Description

Starting with a flakey crust and filling with a delicious blueberry compote, this will satisfy any blueberry lover.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite pie crust - This is a good one Homemade buttery pie crust
  • Blueberries
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Egg wash - 1 large egg beaten with 1Tbsp milk
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Cinnamon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemon Zest

Steps

  1. Prepare pie crust.
  2. Mix and lightly smash blueberries with sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice and zest, along with the cinnamon in a bowl until it no longer looks dry and powdery.
  3. Preheat oven to 425F or 218C.
  4. Roll out pie dough and place on pie dish.
  5. Pour and spread filling evenly into pie dish.
  6. Roll out second pie dough and cover pie or get fancy and create a lattice. Trim excess.
  7. Brush with egg wash
  8. Bake for 25 min, then reduce temp to 375F or 190C and cover edge of crust with aluminium foil to keep it from overcooking. Continue to bake for another 40 to 50 minutes until the pie is bubbling everywhere.
  9. Remove from oven and place on cooling rack. Allow to cool for 4 hours before slicing. The pie will be runny if cut while warm.
  10. Enjoy for up to 5 days stored in the refrigerator.
